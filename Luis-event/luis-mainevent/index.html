We Do Events!



"Events page for your business or activities."



Luis Events is an app where you can book for an event in whatever state and see the attendance of several events in different states.


Array of objects of event data displays the following:

  • Total attendance by City
  • Average Attendance by City
  • Least attended event by City
  • Most attended event by City
  • Show these stats for All Events
